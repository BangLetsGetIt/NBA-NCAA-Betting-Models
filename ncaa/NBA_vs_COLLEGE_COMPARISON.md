## üí° Why College Basketball is Sharper for Betting

### 1. **Line Inefficiency**
College basketball has 350+ teams vs NBA's 30. Books can't be as sharp on every line:
- Mid-major games often have stale lines
- Conference games are harder to price correctly
- Less betting volume = less line correction

### 2. **Public Bias**
Recreational bettors overvalue:
- Duke, Kentucky, North Carolina (brand names)
- Recent national championship winners
- Teams in major media markets
- This creates value on their opponents

### 3. **Larger Edges Available**
- NBA: Average value bet edge = 2-3 points
- College: Average value bet edge = 3-5 points
- More games = more opportunities to find mistakes

### 4. **Conference Knowledge**
If you focus on specific conferences:
- Build expertise books don't have
- Understand team matchups deeply
- Spot situational advantages
